Some Random Information

The school’s motto, "Draco dormiens nunquam titillandus," translates from Latin to "Never tickle a sleeping dragon."

The Forbidden Forest is one of the boundaries, but the grounds also include expansive lakes, cliffs, and even mountains.

Not only does the Room of Requirement appear when a person truly needs it, but it also keeps a hidden record of what people have used it for.

There are 142 staircases in Hogwarts, and some have a mind of their own.
